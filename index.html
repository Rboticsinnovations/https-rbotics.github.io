About Us

Rbotics Innovations Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking technology company building solutions across marine, aerial, and ground platforms. We specialize in developing unmanned systems, cutting-edge electronics, and offer complete solutions for both industrial and defense sectors.

Our expertise includes custom Li-ion battery design, PCB layout & assembly, and full-scale robotic system development—from ROVs and UAVs to rugged ground vehicles.

With a strong commitment to indigenous innovation, we aim to serve the Indian Navy, Air Force, and Military with scalable, reliable, and high-performance technology.

What We Do

  • Marine Systems: ROVs, thrusters, leak detection, depth control, underwater communication
  • Aerial Platforms: Custom UAVs for mapping, surveillance, and autonomous flight
  • Ground Vehicles: Land robots for inspection, mobility, and payload operations
  • Battery Technology: Li-ion packs (3.7V – 72V) for robotics and high-drain systems
  • Electronics: PCB design, embedded systems, layout, fabrication & assembly
  • System Integration: End-to-end integration of mechanical, electrical & software systems

Our Vision

To be a trusted defense technology partner by delivering indigenous, innovative unmanned solutions for India’s strategic sectors.
